## Spirit of This License

Kasvu is an open philosophy. It is shared freely because its value grows when more people can use it, challenge it, build on it, and carry it forward.

This license is not designed to restrict honest use. It is designed to ensure that the philosophy remains open, that its origins are respected, and that no
single actor can enclose what was built to remain a commons.

If you are unsure whether your use requires a conversation, reach out. The answer is almost always yes if the intent is genuine and the use is aligned
with the spirit of Kasvu.
