## During session (shared with the team)

### Part 1: What grew this quarter

**What did we learn this quarter that we did not know at the start?**

**Where did we see the system get stronger, not just busier?**

**Who grew in a way that surprised us?**

**What experiment worked, even partially?**

### Part 2: What did not grow

**What problem came up again this quarter that also came up last quarter?**

**Where did we add activity but not progress?**

**What did we say was a priority that the system did not actually treat as one?**

**Where are we sculpting when we should be cultivating?**

**Key themes from this section:**

---

### Part 3: Where the wind blows

**What is changing in our environment that we have not fully named yet?**

**What are our customers expecting now that they were not expecting six months ago?**

**What is becoming less relevant that we are still investing in?**

**What opportunity is becoming visible that we have not moved on yet?**

**Where does the wind blow to?**

**Key themes from this section:**

---

### Part 4: One thing to cultivate

**What condition does the team agree to deliberately cultivate next quarter?**

**What does it look like right now?**

**What would it look like if it were genuinely healthy?**

**What is one specific change we will make to tend it?**

**Who will take responsibility for keeping it visible?**

**How will we know it is growing?**

**What would signal it is being neglected?**
